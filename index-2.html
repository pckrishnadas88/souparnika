Why choose us

  • Easy accessibility to Sri Chottanikkara Bhagavathy Temple.
  • Wheelchair accessible entrance.
  • Two air- conditioned halls with separate dining areas.
  • Able to accommodate nearly a 1000 in Souparnika Hall.
  • Seating capacity of 350 in the large dining hall.
  • Facility to cater to at least 200 in Raja Rajeswari Hall, with a dining area of up to 100 individuals.
  • A spacious lawn for outdoor celebrations.
  • Stage decor and ambience.
  • Echo proof sound system.
  • Live display of functions in and outside of the hall.
  • A team of dedicated and traditional caterers.
  • Abundant parking facility.
  • Round the clock CCTV surveillance.
  • Bespoke packages that include decorators and caterers, with additional options of photographers, orchestra (both classical and traditional), accommodation and make-up artists.
