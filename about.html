About Us

With nearly two decades worth of experience in hosting weddings and other ceremonies, we endeavour in making your wishes come to life. The venue boasts of two separate auditoriums (Souparnika and Raja Rajeswari), which can successfully cater to both, small and large events, along with a luscious green lawn (The Greens) for outdoor functions. Both the halls are ideal for all types of occasions and conferences.


We understand that parking vehicles at any venue could be an ordeal for the guests. Our grounds have ample tiled parking space with fully dedicated personnel to oversee a systematic and smooth flow of traffic. The bride and groom are also provided with fully air-conditioned en-suite changing rooms.

Above all, we have a team of enthusiastic and creative individuals to execute your special day with meticulous perfection.
